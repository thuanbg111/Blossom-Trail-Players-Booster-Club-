Blossom Trail Players Booster Club

We are a California nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our purpose is to support student‑athletes through fundraising, equipment and travel grants, academic and wellness resources, and community events.

Learn more See programs

What We Do

We rally families, alumni, and partners to lift up our players.

  • Team funding for gear, uniforms, travel, and tournament fees
  • Academic tutoring & wellness resources for student‑athletes
  • Community events that build spirit and celebrate achievement

Funding & Equipment

Mini‑grants for teams and coaches, new gear drives, and travel scholarships.

Events & Spirit

Boosters nights, showcases, senior celebrations, and parent engagement.

Volunteer Network

Parents and alumni coordinating concessions, scorekeeping, and logistics.
