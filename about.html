About Blossom Trail Players Booster Club

Blossom Trail Players Booster Club operates for charitable and public purposes as a recognized 501(c)(3) (EIN 84-3637691).

Mission: to support student‑athletes through fundraising, equipment and travel grants, academic and wellness resources, and community events. We partner with coaches, schools, families, and local businesses to give every player the chance to compete and thrive.

  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable and educational purposes; upon dissolution they pass to a 501(c)(3).
